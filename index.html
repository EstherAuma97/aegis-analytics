06 — FAQ

Common
questions.

The questions we get asked most often before a first call.

Do I need to have my data organised before we start?+
No. Most of our clients come to us because their data is messy or scattered. The Discovery Sprint is designed to assess exactly where you are. You do not need to prepare anything before our first call.
How are you different from a freelancer or a large agency?+
Freelancers are often narrow specialists. Large agencies are expensive and slow, built for enterprise clients. Aegis sits in the middle — a small, senior team with broad capability across data, AI, finance, and security, priced for your stage. You work directly with the people doing the work, not account managers.
What if I am not sure which service I need?+
That is exactly what the free Discovery call is for. Tell us what is not working and we will tell you honestly where we can help and what it would realistically cost. No upselling, no pressure.
How long does a typical project take?+
A Discovery Sprint takes two weeks. A full project engagement typically runs four to eight weeks. We always agree on scope and timeline before you commit to anything.
Do you work with clients outside the United States?+
Yes. We are California-based but work globally. We have particular experience with African and emerging markets and are comfortable across time zones. All engagements can be fully remote.
Is my data secure when working with you?+
Yes. We sign NDAs and data processing agreements before any engagement begins. We never share client data with third parties and treat confidentiality as a baseline, not an add-on.
