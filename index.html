06
FAQ

Questions we get
asked most often.

Do I need to have my data organised before we start?+
No. Most of our clients come to us specifically because their data is messy or scattered. The Discovery Sprint is designed to assess exactly where you are. You do not need to do any prep work before our first call.
How is Aegis different from hiring a freelancer or a big agency?+
Freelancers are often narrow specialists. Large agencies are expensive, slow, and built for enterprise clients. Aegis sits in the middle: a small, senior team with broad capability across data, AI, finance, and security, priced for businesses at your stage. You get direct access to the people doing the work, not account managers.
What if I am not sure which service I need?+
That is exactly what the free Discovery call is for. Book a 30-minute conversation, tell us what is not working, and we will tell you honestly where we can help and what it would realistically take. No upselling, no pressure.
How long does a typical project take?+
A Discovery Sprint takes two weeks. A full project engagement typically runs four to eight weeks depending on scope. We scope carefully at the start and give you a realistic timeline before you commit to anything.
Do you work with clients outside the United States?+
Yes. We are California-based but work with clients globally. We have particular experience with African and emerging markets and are comfortable working across time zones. All engagements can be conducted fully remotely.
Is my data secure when working with you?+
Yes. We follow data handling best practices and can sign NDAs and data processing agreements before any engagement begins. We never share client data with third parties and treat confidentiality as a baseline, not an add-on.
